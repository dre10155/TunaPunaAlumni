Mission Statement

To develop and promote a vibrant network of our Alumni by providing diverse tangible benefits including career mentorship and networking opportunities, supporting our Alma Mater in the holistic development of its members by maintaining lifelong connections while serving its community.

Vision

To champion a lifelong relationship between the school and its alumni. To engage alumni and students through the perpetuation of tradition and pride. As we continue to grow, our organization will strive to increase the offerings of the TSS alumni which will help strengthen the impact we have on our school and allow us to become a strong resource for all TSS students, alumni and staff. The Association will support the school financially and through volunteerism by various endeavours and initiatives including fund raising and public relations efforts. This Association will strive to promote and sustain alumni involvement in school programs and provide social, business, and charitable opportunities for our TSS Alumni to assist students and faculty.
